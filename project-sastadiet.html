In klug inforena, team is dedicated and committed to give 100% to our valuable clients and their projects.

Project : Sasta Diet

Description

SastaDiet is a website which helps users to find out their best diet at very effective cost.

Admin can add dieticians and their charges, and also allocates the new customers.

Dieticians generate the diet plans and send to the customers via emails.

Customers can have two types of accounts: Premium - in which customer can have chat and call both options. Customer need to decide an appointment with dietician and the dietician will create a chatroom where both can discuss about the diet. Normal account allows only chat options to the user.
